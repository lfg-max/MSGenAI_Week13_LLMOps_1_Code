Supply Chain Domain Knowledge

This dataset contains supply chain transaction data including orders, sales, shipping, and inventory information.

Key Metrics:
- Sales: Total revenue from product sales
- Profit: Revenue minus costs
- Order Date: When the order was placed
- Shipping Date: When the product was shipped
- Shipping Mode: Method of shipping (Standard Class, First Class, Second Class, Same Day)
- Customer Segment: Type of customer (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Market: Geographic market region
- Product Category: Category of products
- Order Status: Current status of the order (Complete, Pending, Closed, etc.)

Common Analysis Queries:
- Sales trends over time
- Profitability by product category
- Shipping mode performance
- Customer segment analysis
- Market comparison
- Order fulfillment rates
